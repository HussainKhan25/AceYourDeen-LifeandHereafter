How to Use Digital Tasbih

1
Choose Your Color
Select your preferred tasbih color from the available options. Each color represents a different theme to match your spiritual mood.
2
Select a Dhikr
Click on one of the dhikr options below. Each has its traditional count - SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, and Allahu Akbar are recited 33 times each.
3
Start Counting
Click the circular tasbih counter while reciting your selected dhikr. Each click will increment the count automatically.
4
Complete Your Session
When you reach the target count, you'll see a completion message. The counter will automatically reset for your next session.
